Content Topics

Our channel mainly tackles on the influences of high expectations from our parents/guardians and how it affects our participation in our school activities. We like to share our own experiences and give advice to our fellow learners about a certain topic and how we cope with problems such as this. It is important to be open about topics such as this because it helps us solve the problem.
